Marionette line filler
* Marionette lines are defined as the wrinkles that run from the outer corners of the mouth down to the chin. become more prominent with age 
* often seen at age 40 years old up
* normally use 1-2 cc/session
* most common filler brand for marionette line filler
   * juvederm ultra lasting 12-18 months
   * restylane classic lasting 12-18 months
   * neuramis deep lasting 6-12 months
