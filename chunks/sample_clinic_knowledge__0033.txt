most common side effect after filler
* Swelling
   * Usually worst in the first 24–72 hours
   * Can last longer in areas like lips or under-eyes
* Bruising 
   * Very common because needles/cannulas can hit small blood vessels.
   * More likely if you take alcohol, aspirin, ibuprofen, fish oil, vitamin E, etc.
* Redness and Tenderness 
   * Mild inflammation at injection sites is normal for a few days.
* Firmness or Small Lumps take up to 2 weeks for filler to integrated
