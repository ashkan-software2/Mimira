nasalabial fold fillers (or nasal fold filler),
* Nasolabial folds, often called laugh lines, are the deep creases that run from the sides of the nose down to the corners of the mouth.
* is an injection of dermal filler into the nasolabial folds.
* The treatment helps to solve problems like deep nasolabial folds, obvious wrinkles, and lines that appear when making facial expressions, which can make the face look older or saggy.
* normally use 1-2 cc/sessions
* most common filler brand for nasolabial fold filler
   * juvederm ultra or ulraplus lasting 12-18 months
   * restylane classic lasting 12-18 months
   * neuramis deep lasting 6-12 months
