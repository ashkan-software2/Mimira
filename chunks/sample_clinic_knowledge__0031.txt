Post-Filler Care
* Avoid touching or massaging the treated area. 
* Refrain from consuming alcohol, exercising intensely, 
* Avoid high heat exposure for two weeks. 
* Avoid getting laser treatments or going to a sauna for at least 14 days or one month after your procedure, as extreme heat can cause the filler to dissolve more quickly.
* Drinking plenty of water (1.5 to 2 liters per day) is one of the best things you can do to help your body integrate the filler and keep your skin plump and hydrated.
Serious complications after dermal filler injections that should immediate reach out to clinic
* Increasing severe pain
* Skin turning pale, purple, or black
* Vision changes
