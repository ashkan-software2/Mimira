* First 24–48 Hours Avoid additional heat exposure
   * Sauna
   * Hot yoga
   * Steam rooms
   * Very hot showers
Too much heat can worsen swelling and irritation.
Use: Gentle cleanser, Basic moisturizer, Sunscreen
* Avoid for several days:
   * Retinol/tretinoin
   * AHAs/BHAs
   * Scrubs
   * Strong vitamin C if irritating
   * Harsh acne products
Your skin barrier may be more sensitive temporarily.
* Hydration and moisturization
* Sunscreen is important Use broad-spectrum SPF 30–50 daily.
