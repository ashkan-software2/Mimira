post botox care
First 4 hours: Stay upright. Don’t lie flat, Don’t rub, massage, or press on the treated area.
First 24 hours Avoid: Heavy exercise, Alcohol (can worsen bruising), Sleeping face-down
Avoid facials, gua sha, microcurrent, or tight goggles/glasses pressing on injection sites. Sauna, steam room, hot yoga, very hot showers for two weeks
Common temporary effects for botox:
* Small bumps at injection points for 15–60 minutes
* Mild headache
* Tight/heavy feeling
* Minor bruising
* Slight asymmetry during settling
Results usually:
* Start appearing in 3–5 days
* Peak around 10–14 days
